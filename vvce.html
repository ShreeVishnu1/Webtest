Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering (VVCE), Mysuru

VVCE, established in 1997, is one of the top engineering colleges in Karnataka. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various branches of engineering. The college is affiliated with VTU and accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade.
