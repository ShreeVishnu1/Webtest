"Every journey begins with a small step"

Maistry Thimmaiah Choultry, Irwin Road

Vidyavardhaka sangha initially started a high school in a choultry on Irvin road “Maistry Thimmaiah Choultry”, in 1949. The school used to be declared a holiday on the days of marriages.

Mysore Dasara Exhibition Building

Later as the school was growing and accommodation was a problem the management got the permission to run the school in Mysore Dasara Exhibition building stalls which is now a part of Mysore Medical college and classes were functioning in the stalls with bamboo screen for a long time.

Vidyavardhaka Institutions, Sheshadri Iyer Road

In due course, with efforts of Late Sri K Puttaswamy Founder Secretary and the Minister, Mysore Govt got the own land allotted by then CITB and constructed a two storied building, and shifted the school to present city campus
